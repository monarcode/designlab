Frequently Asked Questions

What will i learn?

  • Learn to recognise & evaluate design, both Good & Bad.
  • Discover how to effectively communicate ideas with others that speaks to your new found understanding of design foundations.
  • Gain a basic understanding of typography, color theory and visual hierarchy.
  • Conceptualize & create components for sites/apps.
  • Gain insightful feedback from peers & professional designers on your progress & work.
  • Come away with a selection of designs for your own portfolio.

WHO IS THIS CLASS FOR?

  • This class is for complete design beginners, who have some familiarity with code(some of our projects involve basic css).
  • Anyone who knows HTML/CSS and wants to learn how design works will feel right at home.
